This dashboard aims to provide information on air quality and help prepare the Town of Cheverly for exposure risks. "Data presented in this dashboard are preliminary and subject to change upon final review."

These measurements are obtained from low-cost sensors and are intended for educational and informational purposes. They are not regulatory-grade instruments and should not be used alone to infer health effects. Use of this data is at the user’s discretion.
