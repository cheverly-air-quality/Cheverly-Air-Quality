Why Should You Use This Dashboard?

Traditional air quality maps often rely on distant regional sensors and focus on a limited list of criteria pollutants. This dashboard provides hyper-local data from sensors placed directly within our neighborhoods to provide targeted and specific estimates of health risks.

By using our CE-AQI (Cumulative Exposure Air Quality Index), we provide a more accurate picture of the air you are actually breathing. Unlike standard tools, the CE-AQI covers high-priority pollutants for overburdened areas like Cheverly, including Black Carbon, diesel particulate matter, and specific volatile organic chemicals. It communicates the health impact of cumulative exposure to multiple criteria and toxic air pollutants at the same time, ensuring you have the real-time information needed to make safe choices for your family.
