Why Should You Use This Dashboard?

Standard air quality maps often rely on distant regional sensors and only track one pollutant at a time. The CheverlyNet Dashboard provides hyper-local data from sensors placed directly within our neighborhoods. By using our CE-AQI (Cumulative Exposure Air Quality Index), which combines PM2.5 and Black Carbon, we provide a more accurate picture of the air you are actually breathing. This ensures you have the real-time information needed to make informed, safe choices for your family.
